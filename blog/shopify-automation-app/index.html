10+ Proven Shopify Automation Apps to Optimize Your Business

“With great power comes great responsibility.” Now we know it doesn’t only apply to superheroes but also to eCommerce store owners. The more your business grows, the busier you are.

There are simply too many tasks to handle when you run an online business. Fulfilling orders, answering questions, tracking profit and costs, keeping your store in perfect condition, and the list goes on.

As tedious yet time-consuming tasks blend with impactful ones, soon you’ll find yourself spending more time fulfilling orders than making strategies.

Don’t let this loop ruin your work-life balance. Luckily, there are already some fantastic Shopify automation apps to help you out.

1. Profit Analytics App

If you’re among the business owner-slash-accountants who spend countless time calculating costs and profit every month, stop it. An eCommerce business deserves a smarter solution. And so do you.

TrueProfit is the financial app every Shopify store owner should have to track real-time profit. It helps you manage all COGS, shipping costs, transaction fees, ad spends, etc. In short, you get a real picture of your business’s financial health.

Furthermore, you could see the product margins of every product; whether a product is truly profitable once other expenses are involved (eg., ads, handling fee, Break Even Point, Profit Margin, etc.).

What makes TrueProfit stand out from other profit-tracking apps is its ability to track your customer profitability analysis, such as LTV, CAC, and LTV:CAC ratio, with absolute precision. 

Only a one-time setup, and the app will automatically pull data from top ad channels, shipping platforms, and all your marketing channels to give you a clear picture of your business performance and all the numbers behind it.

2. Dropshipping Apps

Since dropshipping saves you the hassle of manufacturing, storing, and shipping products, essentially, you need at least one Shopify automation app to automate sourcing products and fulfilling orders.

Currently, the three biggest apps for dropshipping are Dsers, Ali Orders, Dropified, and Syncee.

What can these tools offer?

  • Automated product import: No need to manually add products to your store. You can import from any suppliers in certain apps, not just from AliExpress, along with all images, specifications, and variants.
  • Automated order fulfillment: No more wasted time filling in customer information for every order. With either tool, you can place an order on AliExpress in just a few clicks.
  • Tracking orders: Keep track of every package you’ve sent out and provide customers with up-to-date tracking information when asked.

Oberlo, Dropified, and Syncee apps automate the entire dropshipping business model for you. Each comes at a different price and offers its own special functions.

3. Abandoned Cart Recovery Apps

You’ve spent lots of money gaining traffic to your store, don’t let it go down the drain!

SalesCycle reported that approximately 75.6% of carts were abandoned in 2018. This means you could make much more sales if you successfully recover those lost orders.

Many Shopify apps will help you send tailored messages once visitors leave their cart without paying. Initially, you need to set up an automated flow and design the template. Then it’ll run automatically.

You may have heard about cart abandonment emails, but don’t miss out on other effective channels like Facebook Messenger, push, and SMS.

Our recommended Shopify automation apps for abandoned cart recovery:

  • Emails: SmartrMail Email Marketing
  • Push messages: PushOwl Web Push Notifications
  • Facebook Messenger: Facebook Messenger Marketing
  • All-in-one: Flowio, Omnisend

4. Product Recommendation App

If you’re looking to increase your store’s Average order value, don’t neglect to implement product recommendations, upselling, and cross-selling.

But doesn’t it take time to constantly go back and forth to adjust those recommendations over time? Personalizer by LimeSpot is a great Shopify app to help you automate this process.

This app uses AI to learn from your customer purchase behaviors and industry insights, thus personalizing its recommendations to each customer. It can also be used to cross-sell products that are frequently brought together and upsell after purchase.

5. Chatbot App

If you’re handling customers’ queries, you should’ve realized by now that 60% of the questions were actually repeated and similar to one another. Let chatbots deal with those frequently asked questions. First, it’ll free your time. But more importantly, an instant answer can turn a lost sale into a successful one.

Tidio Live Chat is currently the most prominent Shopify app for automated live chat.

Tidio’s bots will check product availability, inform about delivery status, and give the estimated delivery time.

What else can they offer? Use it to recover abandoned carts, send targeted discounts, reengage visitors who want to leave your site, or highlight a particular product. So many possibilities for you to explore.

Wrapping Up!

That’s it! We’ve just walked you through all the best Shopify apps to help you launch, run, and promote your online store effectively.
